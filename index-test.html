Common questions

What counts as a "contact" and how does billing work?
Your contact count includes all subscribed and unsubscribed contacts stored in your audience. Unsubscribed contacts still count toward your total. Your plan price is set by your highest contact count during the billing period — there are no surprise mid-cycle charges, and you'll only move to a higher tier if your count stays above it.
Does Premium come with a free trial?
No — Essentials and Standard include a free 1-month trial with no credit card required. Premium is a direct purchase with no trial period. If you're evaluating Premium, we recommend talking to our sales team first — they can walk you through the platform for your specific send volume and use case.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll get instant access to new features. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle so you never lose access mid-period. You can manage your plan anytime from your account settings.
How much do I actually save going annual?
Annual billing is charged upfront for the year and saves you approximately 15% compared to monthly billing. For example, Standard at 500 contacts drops from $20/mo to around $17/mo. If you're confident in your current contact tier, annual is the most cost-effective option. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time.
What happens if I exceed my contact limit?
If your contact count exceeds your plan tier, you'll automatically move to the next tier and be billed the difference. You won't be blocked from sending. We'll notify you before any billing change takes effect.
Do you offer nonprofit or partner discounts?
Yes. Verified nonprofits receive a 15% discount on all paid plans. Reach out to our support team with proof of nonprofit status to apply. Additional partner and startup pricing may also be available — contact sales to discuss your situation.
